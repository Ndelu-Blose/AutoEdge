# AutoEdge Purchase Phase Workflow

This document outlines the complete purchase process in the AutoEdge system, detailing each user's actions from initial browsing to purchase completion.

## Overview
The purchase phase involves customers browsing vehicles, making selections, and completing transactions through the AutoEdge platform.

---

## Step-by-Step Purchase Workflow

### 1. **Customer Registration/Login** 🔐
**Who:** Customer  
**Action:** Access the AutoEdge system
- **New Users:**
  - Navigate to registration page
  - Fill out registration form:
    - Personal information (name, email, phone)
    - Address details
    - Create secure password
  - Verify email address
  - Complete profile setup

- **Existing Users:**
  - Navigate to login page
  - Enter email and password
  - Access customer dashboard

**Result:** Customer gains access to the AutoEdge platform

---

### 2. **Vehicle Browsing** 🚗
**Who:** Customer  
**Action:** Explore available vehicles
- Browse vehicle catalog/inventory
- Use search and filter options:
  - Vehicle type (sedan, SUV, truck, etc.)
  - Price range
  - Year, make, model
  - Mileage
  - Features and specifications
- View vehicle details:
  - High-resolution photos
  - Detailed specifications
  - Vehicle history report
  - Pricing information
  - Available financing options

**Result:** Customer identifies vehicles of interest

---

### 3. **Vehicle Selection** ✅
**Who:** Customer  
**Action:** Choose specific vehicle for purchase
- Review detailed vehicle information
- Compare multiple vehicles (if applicable)
- Check vehicle availability
- Review terms and conditions
- Select "Purchase" or "Buy Now" option

**Result:** Customer proceeds to purchase workflow

---

### 4. **Purchase Information Entry** 📝
**Who:** Customer  
**Action:** Complete purchase details
- **Personal Information Verification:**
  - Confirm contact details
  - Update address if needed
  - Provide driver's license information

- **Financial Information:**
  - Choose payment method:
    - Credit/Debit card
    - Bank transfer
    - Financing options
  - Enter payment details securely
  - Review total cost breakdown:
    - Vehicle price
    - Taxes
    - Fees
    - Insurance (if applicable)

- **Additional Options:**
  - Extended warranty
  - Additional insurance coverage
  - Accessories or add-ons

**Result:** All purchase information is collected and verified

---

### 5. **Contract Review and Agreement** 📋
**Who:** Customer  
**Action:** Review and accept purchase terms
- Review purchase contract details:
  - Vehicle specifications
  - Total purchase price
  - Payment terms
  - Delivery options
  - Return/refund policy
  - Warranty information
- Read terms and conditions carefully
- Accept legal agreements
- Provide electronic signature

**Result:** Legal purchase agreement is established

---

### 6. **Payment Processing** 💳
**Who:** Customer + System  
**Action:** Complete financial transaction

**Customer Actions:**
- Confirm payment details
- Authorize payment processing
- Provide additional verification if required (2FA, OTP)

**System Actions:**
- Process payment through secure gateway
- Verify funds availability
- Complete transaction
- Generate payment confirmation

**Result:** Payment is successfully processed and confirmed

---

### 7. **Purchase Confirmation** ✅
**Who:** System + Customer  
**Action:** Finalize purchase completion

**System Actions:**
- Generate purchase confirmation number
- Create contract record in database
- Update vehicle inventory status
- Send confirmation email to customer
- Generate purchase receipt

**Customer Actions:**
- Review confirmation details
- Save confirmation number for records
- Download/print receipt
- Note next steps for delivery

**Result:** Purchase is officially completed and documented

---

### 8. **Post-Purchase Setup** 🎯
**Who:** Customer  
**Action:** Prepare for vehicle delivery
- Access customer dashboard
- Review purchase history
- Prepare for delivery scheduling:
  - Consider delivery preferences
  - Plan availability for delivery
  - Prepare required documentation

**Result:** Customer is ready to proceed to delivery phase

---

## User Roles and Responsibilities

### **Customer Responsibilities:**
- Provide accurate personal and financial information
- Review all terms and conditions thoroughly
- Ensure payment method has sufficient funds
- Maintain communication for any verification needs
- Keep confirmation details for future reference

### **System Responsibilities:**
- Maintain secure payment processing
- Provide accurate vehicle information
- Generate proper documentation
- Send timely confirmations and receipts
- Update inventory and records accurately

---

### **Personal Information:**

- Phone number
- Physical address
- Driver's license number
- Date of birth

### **Financial Information:**
- Payment method details
- Billing address

### **Vehicle Selection:**
- Specific vehicle identification
- Desired options/accessories
- Delivery preferences
- Insurance requirements

### **Payment Security:**

### **Transaction Verification:**

---

## Common Purchase Scenarios

### **Cash Purchase:**
1. Select vehicle
2. Enter personal information
3. Choose full payment option
4. Process payment
5. Complete purchase

### **Financed Purchase:**
1. Select vehicle
2. Apply for financing
3. Wait for credit approval
4. Review financing terms
5. Accept loan agreement
6. Process down payment
7. Complete purchase

### **Trade-In Purchase:**
1. Select new vehicle
2. Provide trade-in vehicle information
3. Get trade-in valuation
4. Apply trade-in value to purchase
5. Process remaining balance
6. Complete purchase

---

## Error Handling and Support

### **Common Issues:**
- Payment processing failures
- Credit approval delays
- Vehicle availability changes
- Technical system errors

### **Resolution Steps:**
- Contact customer support immediately
- Provide purchase confirmation number
- Have payment and personal information ready
- Follow support team guidance

### **Support Channels:**
- Online chat support
- Phone support hotline
- Email support tickets
- FAQ and help documentation

---

## Next Steps After Purchase

Once the purchase phase is complete, customers will:
1. Receive purchase confirmation and receipt
2. Access delivery scheduling options
3. Prepare required documentation for delivery
4. Await contact from delivery team
5. Proceed to delivery phase workflow

---

## Important Notes

- **Purchase Completion:** All purchases must be fully paid before delivery scheduling
- **Documentation:** Keep all confirmation numbers and receipts for future reference
- **Timeline:** Purchase processing typically takes 1-2 business days
- **Changes:** Contact support immediately for any needed changes after purchase
- **Cancellation:** Review cancellation policy before completing purchase
