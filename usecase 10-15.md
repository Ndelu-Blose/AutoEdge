# USE CASES 10 – 15  
AutoEdge Vehicle Service Module (Increment 2)

| # | Use-Case Name | Short Description | Triggering Event | Primary Actors | Preconditions | Main Flow (abridged) |
|---|---------------|-------------------|------------------|----------------|---------------|------------------------|

10 | **Book Service** | Customer books a vehicle-service appointment by selecting service type, date, time and supplying vehicle details. System confirms booking and notifies relevant parties. | Customer accesses booking system via web | Customer, System | 1. Customer has account or proceeds as guest.<br>2. Service centre has available slots. | 1. Customer opens booking page.<br>2. System lists service types (maintenance / repairs / inspection).<br>3. Customer enters vehicle details & picks preferred slot.<br>4. System calculates estimate.<br>5. Customer confirms; system e-mails reference number.<br>6. If no slot available, system offers next dates or wait-list. |

11 | **Service Scheduling** | System lets service manager schedule work by assigning an available mechanic & slot while avoiding double-bookings. | Service manager selects “Schedule Service” on dashboard | Service Manager, System | 1. Service request exists & approved.<br>2. Calendar has free slots.<br>3. Mechanic available. | 1. Manager selects booking.<br>2. Chooses mechanic & date/time.<br>3. System verifies availability.<br>4. Calendar updated; mechanic notified. |

12 | **Vehicle Pickup & Drop-off** | Handles door-to-door collection & return of customer vehicle as part of the service appointment. | Customer ticks “Pickup / Drop-off” option or phones advisor | Customer, Driver, System | 1. Customer has account / existing booking.<br>2. Centre offers logistics service.<br>3. Address within serviceable zone. | 1. Customer requests service & supplies address/time.<br>2. System checks driver availability.<br>3. Driver assigned; customer & driver receive confirmation.<br>4. Driver collects vehicle, uploads condition photos.<br>5. After service, system schedules return; driver delivers & obtains sign-off. |

13 | **Vehicle Check-In & Inspection** | Authorised staff scan vehicle QR code on arrival, verify identity and perform a multi-point inspection (condition, mileage, damage, fuel). Results stored digitally. | Inspector scans QR code or clicks “Check-In Vehicle” | Inspector / Staff, System, Admin (for disputes) | 1. Vehicle pre-registered with unique QR.<br>2. Inspector authenticated.<br>3. Inspection checklist configured. | 1. Opens module & scans QR.<br>2. System displays vehicle data.<br>3. Inspector records odo, exterior/interior photos, damage, fuel, fluids.<br>4. System saves report & updates status to “Under Inspection”.<br>5. Digital report archived; notifications sent. |

14 | **Service Execution** | Assigned technician views work order, performs approved maintenance / repairs, records parts & labour, conducts quality checks, and marks job complete for advisor approval. | Technician clicks assigned job on service dashboard | Technician, Service Advisor, System | 1. Vehicle checked-in & inspected.<br>2. Job scheduled & assigned.<br>3. Parts / tools available. | 1. Technician opens job; sees checklist & tasks.<br>2. Updates progress status (In-Progress / Parts Ordered / Completed).<br>3. Logs parts used, labour hours, extra findings.<br>4. If extra work needed, requests advisor approval.<br>5. On finish, marks “Completed”; advisor reviews & approves for billing. |

15 | **Customer Notification & Payment** | After final approval system automatically notifies customer that service is finished and supplies invoice total. Customer pays securely online; status updated to Paid. | System detects service status = Completed & approved for billing | Customer, System, Service Advisor | 1. Service execution completed & approved.<br>2. Invoice & amount generated. | 1. System auto-generates invoice.<br>2. E-mail / SMS sent: “Vehicle ready – payment due”.<br>3. Customer logs in, selects payment method (card/EFT/cash).<br>4. Payment processed; system records transaction.<br>5. Digital receipt sent; service status set to Paid. |
